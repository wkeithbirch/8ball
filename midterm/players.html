player handicap ratings

To even the playing field we evaluate each player's skill level using a unique handicapping system.
As a new player your handicap starts as a 3 for woman or a 4 for men. These are non-rated handicaps.
After your first 9 games played you will receive your beginning ranking as a rated player and eventually
added to the official ratings book.
